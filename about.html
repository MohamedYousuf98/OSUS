OSUS's Vision for Limitless Possibilities

A Legacy of Transformation. Beyond Buildings

“OSUS” is a Saudi company specializing in real estate development, headquartered in Riyadh. Founded in 2006, the company is committed to creating profitable and exceptional projects ranging from residential and commercial developments to raw lands. To date, more than 11 residential and commercial projects have been delivered, covering a total area of 51,000 square meters and built-up areas exceeding 130,000 square meters.“OSUS” cares about the environment and sustainability, therefore all projects include vast green spaces and sustainable engineering solutions. Real estate represents a vital link that we cherish; it is a meeting point where life intersects with spaces, where innovation meets tradition, and where the earth pulses with life.

Contributing to the Kingdom's ambitious vision and the transformative goals of Vision 2030, our commitment extends beyond buildings, to create a harmonious connection between place, people, and community for a higher quality of life. Through an outstanding project portfolio, we offer exceptional real estate solutions that meet market demands and the future expectations of our clients. We excel in the language of architecture, a blend of design symphony filled with emotion, place, and community, the poetic language of nature, and the intricate dialogue of architecture through time.

“We develop and build for a better tomorrow for the next generation.”
